Empowered Futures

Empowered Futures is a Canadian non-profit organization based in Calgary, AB, dedicated to supporting young adults through business mentorships, life skills workshops, and onine resources. Their mission is to empower individuals facing personal and psychological challenges by helping them develop confidence and practical skills for the future.

As a Frontend Developer, I contributed to the implementation of the new design of the organization's official website. My work involved building reusable and maintainable React components while translating UI/UX designs into fully responsive and accessible interfaces. I utilized React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS to deliver a modern single-page application that reflects the organization's mission. The updated site features both light and dark modes, enhanced accessibility, and a sleek, futuristic visual style that delivers an engaging experience for users across all devices.

Designed and coded with by Kseniia Priakhina. Built with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.
